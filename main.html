A tribute to frontline corona warriors - Doctors

COVID-19 is one of the most dangerous challenges the worldhas faced. When the whole world was suffering, our doctors stood by us, became our saviors and motivated us to stay strong. A set of heroes worked tirelessly in hospitals as healthcare workers battled to control the spread of the virus. But in the fight against this virus, this section of workers suffered irreparable loss of life.Today, more than 2.75 million have lost the battle against this disease.
